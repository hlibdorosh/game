Instructions

How to Play

Use W, A, S, D or arrow keys to move.

On mobile, tilt your device to move the character.

1. Collect 5 gold coins to pass. (Gold coins make you grow)

2. Collect 10 silver coins to pass.

3. Feed 3 rabbits to pass. (press space to throw carrots)

4. Try to avoid the rabbits for 15 sec, they will eat you!
